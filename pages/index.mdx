# slimplate

Welcome to the documentation site for Slimplate, the git integrated content management system (CMS) designed to simplify your workflow and streamline your website development process. With Slimplate, you can create and manage your website's content in a seamless, intuitive manner, thanks to its easy-to-use interface and powerful features.

Slimplate is the perfect tool for building dynamic, responsive websites quickly and efficiently. By leveraging the power of Git, Slimplate allows you to easily collaborate with team members and keep track of changes to your content, ensuring that your website is always up-to-date and accurate.

This documentation site is designed to provide you with all the information you need to get started with Slimplate and start building amazing websites. From installation and configuration to customization and advanced features, our documentation covers everything you need to know to make the most of Slimplate.

See [Quick Start](quickstart), to get started.
